Responsible AI Principles


Fairness

Ensuring equitable treatment for all users.

Transparency

Clear visibility into how algorithms make decisions.

Accountability

Responsibility for decisions made by AI.

Privacy

Respecting user data and privacy well.

Security

Protecting user data from unauthorized access.

Human-Centric

AI should enhance human capabilities, not replace them.

Reliability

AI systems should perform consistently and reliably.

Inclusivity

AI should be accessible to all users, regardless of background.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally friendly practices in AI.

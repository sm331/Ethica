What Are The Principles?

Fairness

Ensuring equitable treatment for all users.

✅ Avoid bias in data and algorithms.

We strive for algorithms that do not favor one group over another.

Transparency

Clear visibility into how algorithms make decisions.

✅ Users should understand AI behaviors.

We provide clear documentation and disclosure of AI processes.

Accountability

Responsibility for decisions made by AI.

✅ Clear lines of accountability in AI systems.

We ensure that every decision can be traced back to a responsible entity.

Privacy

Respecting user data and privacy well.

✅ Minimize data collection.

We implement strong privacy measures to protect user information.

Security

Protecting user data from unauthorized access.

✅ Implement strong security measures.

We utilize encryption and other technologies to safeguard data.

Human-Centric

AI should enhance human capabilities, not replace them.

✅ Focus on user benefits and experiences.

We prioritize designs that empower users and enrich their lives.

Reliability

AI systems should perform consistently and reliably.

✅ Continuous testing and improvement.

We ensure our systems are rigorously tested before deployment.

Inclusivity

AI should be accessible to all users, regardless of background.

✅ Design for diverse user needs.

We consider all user perspectives to create inclusive solutions.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally friendly practices in AI.

✅ Reduce carbon footprint.

We aim to create AI solutions that are energy-efficient and sustainable.

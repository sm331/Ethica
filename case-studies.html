Case Study One

The Challenge

A bank implemented AI to reduce transaction delays for customers, but faced challenges with bias in training data, leading to discrimination against certain groups.

Potential Harms

  • Bias impacting disadvantaged groups
  • Increased financial transaction errors
  • Trust issues with customers

The Solution

Use diverse training data to reduce bias and implement regular audits for fairness in AI decisions.

Key Takeaways

  • Balance security while ensuring equitable deployment.
  • Engage stakeholders in AI development.
  • Continuously monitor AI outcomes for fairness.

Case Study Two

The Challenge

An AI system in hiring faced accusations of bias against candidates from specific demographics, impacting the fairness of the hiring process.

Potential Harms

  • Reduced diversity in the workforce
  • Legal implications for discriminatory practices
  • Negative public perception of the company

The Solution

Implement regular audits of the AI algorithm and involve diverse teams in the hiring process.

Key Takeaways

  • Regularly review AI systems to ensure fairness.
  • Encourage diverse perspectives in algorithm development.
  • Foster transparency in hiring practices.

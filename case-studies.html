AI Ethics Case Studies

Bias in Hiring Algorithms

The Challenge: A major tech company used AI-powered hiring software that showed bias against female applicants.

Potential Harms: Unfair hiring practices, gender discrimination, and legal risks.

The Solution: Implemented fairness audits, improved training data diversity, and added human oversight.

Key Takeaways: AI should be regularly tested for bias, and human decision-making should complement automated systems.

Facial Recognition and Privacy

The Challenge: A government agency deployed facial recognition AI for security but raised concerns about privacy violations.

Potential Harms: Privacy invasion, wrongful identification, and surveillance overreach.

The Solution: Introduced transparency policies, gave users opt-out options, and enforced strict data usage guidelines.

Key Takeaways: AI policies should be transparent, and users should have control over their data.
